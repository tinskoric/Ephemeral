# Ephemeral

A browser extension for temporarily saving Wikipedia (or other) deep dives. Use this when you want to save something in the short-ish term, but don't want it to sit in your bookmarks forever and don't want it to crowd your tabs. Tabs saved will automatically be cleared when they are older than the expiry time (defaults to 7 days) or if there are more tabs saved than the max (defaults to 32 tabs). There is also a whitelist determining which sites *can* be saved (defaults to just Wikipedia links); the idea here is so you don't just use this for saving everything, but you can get around it by just adding something like `*.com` or `*.org` to it. You can change any of these things in the preferences page wherever you manage your other extensions. To save a page (assuming it is whitelisted), right-click on the page or its tab and select "Ephemeral Save."
